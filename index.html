FAQ

Common questions before you commit to a launch system.

What do you mean by “launch system”?
Biz’n’Box includes the **plan**, the **documents**, and the **training** — plus the website. Instead of “a site and good luck,” you get a repeatable way to operate.
Do you customize the templates to my business?
Yes. We start with proven patterns, then tailor them to your offer, your customers, and how you deliver.
Is the website optional?
If you already have a site, we can integrate with it. If you don’t, we’ll ship a clean web presence that matches your plan and toolkit.
What do you need from me to start?
A short description of what you sell, your target customers, and any existing brand assets. We’ll handle the structure and help draft missing copy.
